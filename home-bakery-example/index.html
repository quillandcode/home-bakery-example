About Us / Our Story

Welcome to Celina Loaf & Hearth, where every loaf and pastry is baked with a generous sprinkle of love and a dash of tradition. I'm Gertrude, and my kitchen is my happy place, filled with the comforting aromas of fresh bread and sweet treats. What started as a passion for baking for family and friends has blossomed into a small, heartfelt business dedicated to bringing wholesome, delicious homemade goodies to our community.

From the first knead to the final golden crust, I pour my heart into every creation, using only the finest ingredients. It's more than just baking; it's about sharing joy, one delicious bite at a time. Thank you for supporting a local dream built on warmth, flavor, and a whole lot of love!
